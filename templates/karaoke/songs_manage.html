Finding Embeddable Karaoke Videos
Important: Many YouTube videos have embedding restrictions and won't work in the karaoke player.
How to Find Embeddable Videos
  1. Search for karaoke versions: Instead of the original song, search:
    [Song Name] [Artist] karaoke
    Example: "Bohemian Rhapsody Queen karaoke"
  2. Look for karaoke channels: Videos from dedicated karaoke channels usually allow embedding:
    • Sing King
    • Karaoke Version
    • Lucky Voice Karaoke
    • KaraFun Karaoke
  3. Check if embedding is allowed:
    • Go to the YouTube video
    • Click "Share" button
    • Click "Embed"
    • If you see embed code, it's safe to use ✅
    • If you see a restriction message, choose another video ❌
Videos to Avoid
  • Official music videos from record labels (often restricted)
  • Live performance recordings (frequently restricted)
  • Videos with copyright claims
  • User uploads with "This video may contain content from..." warnings
Best Practices

✓ Good Example:

"Bohemian Rhapsody - Queen (Karaoke Version)" from Sing King channel

✗ Bad Example:

"Queen - Bohemian Rhapsody (Official Video Remastered)" from Queen channel

Tip: If you get an embedding error after adding a song, simply edit it and replace the YouTube URL with a karaoke version that allows embedding.
